Real-World Examples of AR/VR Applications

Healthcare Training Simulation using VR:

Context: Hospitals and medical institutions utilize VR simulations for training purposes.
Details: Surgeons and medical students can practice surgeries in a controlled, risk-free environment, improving skills and decision-making. Challenges include ensuring accurate and realistic simulations and the high initial cost of VR equipment.

Retail AR Applications:

Context: Retailers integrate AR into their platforms to enhance customer experiences.
Details: AR-enabled apps allow users to visualize products like furniture in their homes before purchasing. Challenges include the need for high-quality 3D models, seamless integration with the shopping process, and ensuring a user-friendly interface.
