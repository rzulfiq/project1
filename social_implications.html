Critical Discussion of Technology and Applications

Social Implications:

Ethical Lens 1 - Privacy Concerns:

AR/VR technologies often collect and use user data for personalization and tracking.
Concerns arise regarding data privacy, consent, and potential misuse of personal information.

Ethical Lens 2 - Inclusivity and Accessibility:

VR headsets, in particular, may exclude individuals with certain disabilities.
Ensuring inclusive design and accessibility features remains a challenge in AR/VR development.
