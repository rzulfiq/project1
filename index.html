AR/VR Technology

Introduction:

AR overlays digital information onto the real world, enhancing real-world experiences by adding virtual elements, often viewed through smartphones or AR glasses.
VR immerses users in a completely artificial environment, usually experienced through specialized headsets, creating a sense of presence and interaction.

Justification for Choosing:
AR/VR technologies are revolutionizing various sectors, including education, healthcare, entertainment, and retail, by offering immersive and interactive experiences. These technologies hold the potential to redefine how people interact with digital content and each other, making them highly relevant in today's tech-driven world.
